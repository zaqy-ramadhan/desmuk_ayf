Women Preneurs

21st March, 2019

The ASEAN Youth Organization strongly believes in the importance of encouraging and supporting young people’s and in particular young women’s aspirations. With this in mind, The ASEAN Youth Organization respectively organized a forum with the theme of “Womenpreneurs for ASEAN Development” in collaboration with the Canadian Mission to ASEAN.

Programs

Conferences

ASEAN Youth Conference Program is a conference aimed at bringing together young people from ASEAN countries to discuss various issues and challenges faced by the region.

Forum

Online Forum of ASEAN Youth Forum. Online Forum is a forum for young southeast asians used for online discussions.

Workshop

Workshop to Improve skill of ASEAN YOUTH. ASEAN Youth Workshop Program is a program aimed at improving the skills and capabilities of young people in ASEAN countries in leadership, innovation, entrepreneurship, and technology-based jobs.

Yuwana Zine

Yuwana Art Program by ASEAN Youth Forum. Yuwana is derived from Sanskrit word yauvana meaning youth. It stands for young, uniting, witty, accessible, and non-discriminating activism. We hope through this zine-making process, we could build solidarity among youth in the region.
