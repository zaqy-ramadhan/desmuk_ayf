GRIEFF, LOSS, AND HUMAN RIGHTS

Friday - Saturday, 9 - 10 December 2022

Grief and loss are two things that are very personal and can affect a person's life significantly. However, when grief and loss are related to violations of human rights, the impact becomes more complex and can extend to society as a whole. The exhibition with the theme Grief, Loss, and Human Rights aims to show how grief and loss are related to human rights violations, as well as their impact on individuals and society.


By presenting works of art and panel discussions, this exhibition with the theme Grief, Loss, and Human Rights aims to increase awareness and understanding of the importance of human rights and the impact that occurs when these rights are violated. This exhibition is also expected to provide space for dialogue and reflection, as well as inspire action and positive change in society.
