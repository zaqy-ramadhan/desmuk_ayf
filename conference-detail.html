In conjunction with the ASEAN Day 2022, AYO Germany and the AYO United Kingdom co-organized ASEAN-European Youth Conference 2022 to provide a platform for aspiring youth leaders in ASEAN and Europe to cultivate greater awareness and understanding of pressing issues facing the region today. With AEYC 2022, we want to extend this activity to the long-standing partnership between two regions that offer many similarities and differences. During the conference, delegates will also be tasked to create a policy recommendation held virtually and divided into two main topics:

1. Asean - European Relations
2. Mobility and Nationality
